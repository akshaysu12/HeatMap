HeatMap



Heatmap uses hexagonal binning to help you visualize your League of Legends Data.

By aggregating your recent Match Data, heatmap compiles your death locations into easily viewable hexagons that help you identify where on the rift you are dying the most. Head back to the homepage to try it out!
